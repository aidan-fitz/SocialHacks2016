FAQ

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a weekend long event where participants get together to work on cool projects that solve real world problems or issues. 		Who should attend?
Anyone who is a high school or college student is welcome to attend! Whether you are a beginner or intermediate hacker, people of any level of skill set can attend. 		What do I bring?
Besides your laptop and any necessary technology, you should also bring any toiletries and sleeping bag for the overnight stay! Any food and drinks will be provided by us. :)
How do I form or join a team?
We encourage participants to come with a team beforehand but if you don’t have a team, that’s alright! There’ll be a team networking event where you can meet people like you who are ready to build the next big thing!

If you have any other questions not listed above, feel free to shoot us an email at socialhacksnyc@gmail.com!
