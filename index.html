About

Social Hacks is a 24-hour, student-run hackathon that brings together students, programmers,technologists, and activists in New York City to collaborate on technological solutions to the toughest challenges that humanity faces today—poverty, climate change, global health, cybersecurity, and more!

Want to know more about Social Hacks? Feel free to shoot us an email at socialhacksnyc@gmail.com or reach out to us on our Facebook.
